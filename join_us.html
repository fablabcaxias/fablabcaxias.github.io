Education is one of the main activities of Fab Lab Barcelona

Our Fab Lab offers an engaging learning space, with an accent on knowledge development, not only with the brain but by engaging all senses. We are home to digital fabrication machines, classrooms, event spaces, designers, makers, and inspiring projects.


How to Join

If you are an individual and want to know more or learn:


  • Visit us: Open Days at Fab Lab
  • Become a guru: Fab Academy (6 months)
  • Learn: Bootcamp (1 week)
  • Learn more: Workshop (2 days)
  • Work with us: Internship Program, fill the online form HERE

If you represent a group (small business, corporate organisation, school or university)


  • Bring our mobile Fab Lab to your Event, School, Festival, etc.
  • Rent our space for your class or event
  • Organize activities for your students, employees, clients, etc. and offer then our expertise on digital fabrication and innovation.

Contact us: info@fablabbcn.org
